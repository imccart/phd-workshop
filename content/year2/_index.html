In Year 2, you should begin the transition from student to researcher. You’re still taking some classes, but you need to also generate some research ideas. The purpose of this 2nd year workshop is to share some of best practices and general advice to help you generate research ideas. This is not about how to really do the research (that’s what a PhD is for, after all!). This workshop is also not about workflow, replicability, or transparency in your research…that stuff is covered in the 3rd year workshop.
